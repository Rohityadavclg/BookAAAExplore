About Trip

A trip to Hampi and Gokarna offers a blend of ancient history and beach relaxation, with Hampi featuring the ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire and Gokarna known for its serene beaches and spiritual sites like the Mahabaleshwar Temple.

Day 4:
Murudeshwar | Honnavar | Yana Caves Exploration.
Murudeshwar | Honnavar | Yana Caves Exploration.
Meal – Breakfast, Lunch.
• Wake up early in the morning, Checkout.
• Breakfast on the way.
• Yana Caves: It’s a 1-hour drive from Gokarna. The Yana cave complex consists of two black-coloured ant-hill-shaped gigantic hills named Bhairaveshwar Shikhara and Mohini Shikhara, situated just above the Sahyadri mountain range. Both shikaras, or hills, are created naturally from black karst limestone.
• Depart to Honnavar and visit the Mangrove Forest & Backwater boating.
• Visit Murudeshwar, which has the second-largest Shiva statue.
• Start the return journey to hometown. Dinner on the way.
