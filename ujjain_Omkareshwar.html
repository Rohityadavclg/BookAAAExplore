Ujjain & Omkareshwar

Duration 2 Days / 1 Night
Category Family Tour
Booking Price start from ₹5,999/-* per person

Itinerary
Day 0
Depart from Pune/Mumbai or from your respective city to Ujjain
• Meal – No Meal
• You can take Train or SL Bus to Ujjain from your hometown
• From Pune Train no. 22943 - DAUND INDORE Express
• From Mumbai Train no. 12961 - AVANTIKA EXP
• For Train & Bus bookign Book and Explore team will help you.
Day 1
Reach Ujjain early in the morning
Meal – Breakfast & Lunch.
• Reach Ujjain early in the morning and check-in in hotel. (Washrooms for changing & fresh-up as our check-in time of hotel is 11AM)
• Post Breakfast will head to Mahakal Darshan.
• Darshan time is depend on the rush.
• Post Lunch will visit Kal Bhairav Temple, Mangalnath Temple, Bhartari Caves, Sandipani Aashram, Vikram Kirti Mandir, Harsiddhi shaktipeeth, Ram Ghat Aarti,Harsiddhi Temple, Mahakal Lok - Corridor.
• All the above places are near to each other and the roads are narrow will visit this places by walk or by Auto.
• Will try to cover all the places after darshan but it’ s totally depend on time required to take darshan.
Day 2
Omkareshwar Temple & Indore
Meal – Breakfast & Lunch.
• For Bhasma aarti Booking visit https://shrimahakaleshwar.com/bhasmarti .
• Wakeup early in the morning fresh up & get ready to transfer for Omkareshwar Darshan in AC Sitting Vehicle as per the group size. (Car or SUV, or TT)
• Breakfast on the way.
• Omkareswar Darshan.
• Darshan time is depend on the rush.
• Post Lunch will head to Indore If time permits will visit Ahilyadevi Holkar Wada & Sarafa Bazar.
• Drop at Indore Railway Station.
• You can take Train or SL Bus from Indore to your hometown.
• For Pune Train no is. 22944 - INDB DAUND EXP.
• For Mumbai Train no is. 12962 - AVANTIKA EXP.
Day 3
Arrival in Pune/Mumbai.
