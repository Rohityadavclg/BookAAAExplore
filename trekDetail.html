      Most Frequent Questions

      What is Book & Explore?
      Book & Explore is a leading travel company specializing in trekking, adventure tours, and customized travel experiences across Maharashtra and beyond. With 8+ years of expertise and 10,000+ happy travelers, we are committed to making travel seamless, fun, and unforgettable.
      What kind of trips do you offer?
      ✅ Trekking & Adventure Tours (Maharashtra forts, Spiti Valley, Kedarnath-Badrinath, and more)
      ✅ Cultural & Heritage Tours (Hampi-Gokarna, Rann of Kutch, Kerala, and others)
      ✅ Customized & Group Tours (Tailor-made trips for individuals, families, and corporate groups)
      ✅ Weekend Getaways & Camping (Perfect for quick escapes into nature)
      Who can join your trips?
      Our trips are designed for everyone—solo travelers, couples, families, corporate groups, and adventure enthusiasts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced explorer, we have an itinerary that suits you.
      How do I stay updated on upcoming trips?
      Follow us on social media, WhatsApp, and our website for the latest updates, upcoming trips, and special discounts!
      How do I book a trip?
      📞 Call or WhatsApp us for instant assistance.
      💻 Visit our website and choose your preferred trip.
      📧 Email us for customized plans and corporate bookings.
      What is included in the trip cost?
      ✅ Accommodation (Camps, Hotels, or Homestays)
      ✅ Meals as per the itinerary
      ✅ Local transport & transfers
      ✅ Guided tours & trek leads
      ✅ Safety equipment & first-aid assistance
      Do you offer customized travel packages?
      Absolutely! We specialize in customized itineraries for groups, families, and corporate teams. Just tell us your destination, budget, and preferences, and we’ll craft the perfect journey for you.
      How safe are your treks and tours?
      ✅ First-aid kits & emergency backup
      ✅ Well-planned itineraries with safety checks
      ✅ Assistance for beginners & proper gear recommendations
      What if I need to cancel my trip?
      We understand that plans change. We have a flexible cancellation and refund policy, which depends on the type of trip. Details will be shared at the time of booking.
      Why should I choose Book & Explore?
      🚀 8+ years of expertise in the travel industry
      🌍 10,000+ happy travelers
      🏕 Expert-led treks and tours
      🎯 Personalized travel experiences
      💯 Hassle-free bookings and seamless execution
